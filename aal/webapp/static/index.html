Deterministic Storyboard Planner

Provide a narrative prompt. The planner synthesises a 2 minute storyboard of twenty 6-second segments using the offline Grok Imagine simulator and ChatGPT-inspired script generator.

The harness renders a deterministic WebM recording sourced directly from the canvas preview. Frames remain synthetic and hash-derived so researchers can study timing, captions, and colour palettes without introducing external assets or nondeterministic codecs.
